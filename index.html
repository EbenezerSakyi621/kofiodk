About

  • Read Bio

  • Kofi Odenchem is a talented Ghanaian musician and songwriter making waves in the music industry. With a unique style that blends afrobeats, hip-hop, and highlife, he has captured the attention of music lovers across Africa and beyond. Born and raised in Ghana, Kofi Odenchem's passion for music started at a young age. He began his career in the music industry releasing singles, which quickly gained him a local following. His has released songs like "Trapper Man", "Ly", "Killer Flow", "Sore", "Bmw", and "Dance Floor". His music often features catchy hooks, clever wordplay, and infectious beats, making him a favorite among fans of African music. Kofi Odenchem's talent and hard work have earned him nominations and awards in the Ghanaian music industry. He continues to inspire and entertain his fans with his unique sound and energetic live performances.
